Subscription Settings

The Subscription Settings page allows configuring settings for your KanivinERP subscriptions.

To access the Subscription list, go to:

Home > Accounting > Subscription Management > Subscription Settings

  • Grace Period: Number of days after invoice date has elapsed before canceling subscription or marking subscription as unpaid.

  • Cancel Invoice After Grace Period: Instead of displaying 'Unpaid', the Subscription will be canceled in KanivinERP if the Customer does not pay.
