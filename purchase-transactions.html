Purchase Transactions

Purchase transactions in KanivinERP encompass the various activities involved in acquiring goods or services from suppliers.

These transactions typically include creating purchase orders to specify the items or services needed, receiving goods against these orders to update inventory levels, and processing purchase invoices for payment. Additionally, KanivinERP facilitates communication with suppliers, tracks purchase history, and integrates with accounting modules for seamless financial management.

  • Material Request
  • Purchase Order
  • Purchase Invoice
  • Debit Note
  • Request for Quotation
  • Supplier Quotation
  • Supplier Quotation Through Portal
  • Purchase Return
