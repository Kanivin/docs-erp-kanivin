Point of Sale

The Point of Sale (POS) module in KanivinERP offers a streamlined solution for managing retail transactions efficiently. It seamlessly integrates with inventory management, updating stock levels in real-time and preventing stock discrepancies. KanivinERP's POS also supports multiple payment methods, streamlining the payment process for customers and reducing transaction errors.

With built-in reporting and analytics, businesses can gain valuable insights into sales performance, top-selling items, and customer trends, helping them make informed decisions to improve overall efficiency and profitability in their retail operations.
