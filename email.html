Email Settings

There is a process for configuring email in KanivinERP that can greatly enhance communication and productivity within the system. By configuring your email settings, you can seamlessly send and receive emails directly from KanivinERP which allows efficient correspondence with clients, suppliers, and team members in order to maintain transparency.

Additionally, the users can personalise email templates, set up auto-replies, and schedule email notifications, enhancing productivity and streamlining communication workflows within the ERP system.
